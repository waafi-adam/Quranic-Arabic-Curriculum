# Quranic Arabic Curriculum

**Mission:** Understand every word of the Quran by mastering Grammar (Nahw) and Morphology (Sarf) together.

This curriculum is designed so that a student can look at any ayah and answer two questions about every word:

1. **What role does it play in the sentence?** (Nahw)
2. **Where does it come from and what does its pattern tell me?** (Sarf)

## How to Use This Curriculum

1. **Go in order.** Each phase builds on the previous one.
2. **Memorize the terms.** The Arabic grammar terms are used throughout the Quran's commentaries (tafseer). Knowing them unlocks a lifetime of learning.
3. **Practice on the Quran immediately.** After each lesson, open any page of the Quran and try to identify what you just learned.
4. **Use the Sarf tables as a reference.** You don't need to memorize every table on day one. Come back to them repeatedly.
